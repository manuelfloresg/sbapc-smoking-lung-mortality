## fig_support_window_comparison
Files: fig_support_window_comparison.svg, fig_support_window_comparison.pdf
Title: Observed-window and full-support scenario-effect recovery
Note: Appendix diagnostic comparing simulated truth, observed-window SBAPC, and full-support SBAPC for annual mortality scenario effects relative to the frozen-prevalence baseline. Full-support SBAPC is an oracle-style diagnostic, not a feasible empirical estimator. Source: Own elaboration.

## fig_misspecification_scenario_recovery
Files: fig_misspecification_scenario_recovery.svg, fig_misspecification_scenario_recovery.pdf
Title: Scenario-effect recovery under transmission-rule misspecification
Note: Appendix robustness diagnostic comparing the well-specified design with a misspecified monotone transmission design. Panels show annual mortality scenario effects relative to the frozen-prevalence baseline. Source: Own elaboration.

## fig_reliability_calibration
Files: fig_reliability_calibration.svg, fig_reliability_calibration.pdf
Title: Projection reliability calibration by support horizon
Note: Calibration is treated as an uncertainty-summary diagnostic. The central validation target for Section 4 is recovery of mortality scenario effects, not calibration of predictive summaries. Source: Own elaboration.
